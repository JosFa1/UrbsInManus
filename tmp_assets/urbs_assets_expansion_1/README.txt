URBS IN MANUS EXPANSION I

This pack adds 7 portraits and 3 backgrounds in the same flat SVG style as the starter pack.

DROP-IN PATHS
Place the contents of the assets folder into your project assets folder.

FILES
Portraits
- quartermaster-sabina.svg
- surveyor-ruso.svg
- interpreter-neria.svg
- council-elder-cassian.svg
- scribe-clerk.svg
- veteran-spokesman.svg
- matron-household.svg

Backgrounds
- market-row.svg
- shrine-crossing.svg
- records-office.svg

CANVAS SIZES
- Portraits: 1024x1024
- Backgrounds: 1600x900

STYLE
- Flat fills only
- Dark muted palette
- One main shadow mass per major form
- Lower area of backgrounds kept darker for dialogue readability
- Strong silhouette and face readability at small size

SUGGESTED USE
- quartermaster-sabina: storage, rationing, supply, grain, work crews
- surveyor-ruso: roads, walls, drainage, parcels, boundaries
- interpreter-neria: local disputes, translation, diplomacy, customs
- council-elder-cassian: law, prestige, charters, elite demands
- scribe-clerk: tax rolls, records, census, audit, contracts
- veteran-spokesman: settlers, land grants, discipline, veterans
- matron-household: families, domestic concerns, food, safety

- market-row: trade, stalls, prices, merchants
- shrine-crossing: local rites, omens, tensions, road decisions
- records-office: law, census, treasury, archived petitions
