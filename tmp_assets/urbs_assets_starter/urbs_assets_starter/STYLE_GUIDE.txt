Style rules
- Flat fills only
- No gradients
- 1 main light direction from upper left
- One shadow shape per major form
- Keep lower 25 to 30 percent of backgrounds darker and calmer for text readability
- Faces should stay readable at 128 px
- Backgrounds should read clearly even when heavily cropped on mobile

Suggested project mapping
- governor-tent.svg for briefings, decrees, early authority scenes
- harbor.svg for arrivals, trade, shortages, imports, naval talk
- road.svg for patrols, locals, travel, land routes, unrest
- survey-camp.svg for petitions, planning, allocation, census, early civic scenes
- duovir.svg for player voice or city magistrate
- legate.svg for governor or imperial administration
- centurion.svg for military order, security, force, discipline
- merchant.svg for trade petitions
- augur.svg for religion, omens, sacred complaints
